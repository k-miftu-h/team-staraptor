Our Vision

To become CUET's leading UAV team, advancing drone innovation through engineering excellence and global competition. About METU VTOL Competition

The METU VTOL Competition, hosted by Middle East Technical University (METU), challenges teams to design and build VTOL aircraft under strict engineering and safety standards. Teams complete mission-based tasks suchs as payload delivery, autonomous navigation, and efficient flight.

Participants are evaluated on engineering design, technical documentation, flight performance, safety, and innovation. The competition is internationally recognized and serves as a stepping stone for careers in aerospace, robotics, and UAV development.
